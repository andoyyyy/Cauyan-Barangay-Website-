Services

The barangay offers a variety of services to its residents, including:

  • Health and Wellness Programs: Barangay Canlandog offers regular health services including free medical consultations, vaccination programs, and health seminars to ensure the well-being of the community.
  • Livelihood Assistance: We provide training and resources for small-scale businesses, as well as access to micro-financing options to empower local entrepreneurs.
  • Waste Management and Clean-Up Drives: Our barangay organizes monthly clean-up drives and offers waste segregation and recycling programs to maintain cleanliness and promote environmental awareness.
  • Barangay Peacekeeping: We have an active group of Barangay Tanods to ensure the safety and security of residents, especially during community events and emergencies.
  • Educational Support: We offer scholarships for underprivileged students and provide tutoring programs to help children excel in their studies.
  • Disaster Preparedness: Barangay Canlandog conducts regular disaster preparedness workshops and coordinates relief efforts in times of calamities such as typhoons or floods.
